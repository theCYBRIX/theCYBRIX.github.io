Out of the gutter

A peer-to-peer multiplayer game made using Godot that revolves around slimes in a sewer system competing agains each other in a variety of minigames. The game was conceved and brought to it's current state during a hackathon as a colaboration between Koloss0, nigerianpickle, Kingjoker4life, JordonH03 and myself.
